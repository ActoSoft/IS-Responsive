Meet us

I.S is a Mexican company designed to suit your necessities, with the purpose of offer you security and technology solutions, also in localization and tracking services, such as GPS Management, Mobile Service Management, and more.

Through our powerful server you will monitor in real time your work team, offering a first class service, with the more sophisticated and necessary tools to get a great work-team logistic control, acquiring whole security, and also the confidence in the server, but most important in the company.

I.S focuses mainly on logistics and enterprise assets management, obtaining the best results of the national market, optimizing all the necessary resources to achieve the proposed objectives.

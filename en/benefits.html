Benefits

GPS Localization Platform

Technology and Security Solutions
Satellite Intelligence gives you GPS Software integrated with our powerful server, to provide a first-class service, a business system designed, with broad to cover different market segments, such as Fleet Management, Taxi companies with an APP applicative system, Special Vehicles.
S.I. has an innovation system in constant improvement; you will be receiving available software updates and thus maintain a differentiated service on market allowing fidelity to your industry. S.I. has a design team to support your necessities in specific applications either hardware or software.

Keep your Fleet Located!

The Vehicle location lets you know quickly and efficiently your vehicle’s location 24/7.

Tracking

It allows you to request specific positions, vehicle real-time tracking and view the distance traveled over a period of time.
All available options are customizable and could create different vehicle zones.

Routes

Displays the current path of the vehicle, searches and displays routes, check the stop’s history, control limits, schedule verification and more….

Groups and Waypoints

Clusters of waypoints allow us a quick and visual view of all waypoints groups that are located on the map.

Alarms

Set the mobile numbers to notify alerts. You can crate motion alerts, know when your vehicle is in motion. Security’s Zone Alarm, to know when is inside or outside a determined zone, speed alerts, and more…

Reports

You can view the path followed and the generated alerts in a given period of time, customized reports with export option.

Sensors

With sensors we can know if our vehicle is full, if our cold store does not have the correct temperature, etc.

Security

The CELCOM GPS Locator Service, is a system that provides 24/7 security and active vehicle protection or mobile assets.

Mobile Control

With a mobile with web browser and internet connection (Wi-Fi or 3G) you can stay in tune, anywhere, about where your vehicle is.

Real-Time Tracking

Unattended Monitoring

The vehicle motion can be monitored directly on screen without the necessity of refreshing the page, making an easier online vehicle tracking, current position and the followed route without necessity of choosing any other option on screen, only viewing the motion.

Map Zoom

Map Zoom, with an accuracy up to 3 meters.

GPS Device

The GPS charge is through the vehicle’s power. Other types of power are allowed such as autonomous batteries, lighter connection, etc. The installation cost is minimal.

Driver’s Identification

It can be known, anytime, the vehicle’s driver. By RFID cars or iButton and a reader inside the vehicle, the driver is identified in the system. The responsible could know the time and place where the driver left with the vehicle. With that information, work reports can be generated.

Monitoring and Tracking

Monitoring and Tracking in a map of the location in real time of the fleet, drivers and field operators. The location accuracy is about 2.5 meters.

SMS to Drivers

Distance and positions are registered every minute. (Customizable)

Speed Control

Instantaneous Speed Control of each vehicle in the fleet
SMS can be send to driver’s mobile phone and being registered.

Photographic Satellite

Use the satellite “Photographic View” to control the actual vehicle’s position when off the road. Easy one-click access to most functions, such as sending messages to drivers or vehicle’s routes studies on map.

Time Monitor

Monitor driving times, stop times and break times.

Optimal Routes

Check departure time, arrival time and each stop’s duration along the route.

Trips Abroad

The GPS device allows knowing the position storage when the vehicle is outside the contracted data network coverage (Roaming), could be send to server.

Alarms and Events

The vehicle behavior and driving rules can be configured, allowing a constant vehicle monitor system and alert at the moment a rule was transgressed or when a particular event happened, you could receive these alerts via email, no additional cost, or short SMS (operator cost, in this case the customer must purchase a package of messages in CELCOM GPS).

The alarms can be of different types (sensors, location, time, etc.) Definitely, different type of events or alarms can be configured and set, sensors based on client’s needs.

The area could have any shape either regular or irregular.

Unexpected Shutdown

It’s allowed to receive SMS and email notifications for each unexpected stop along the way with customized duration.

Additional Sensors Alarm

Gates monitor, opening/closing, external control devices activation, a panic button to prevent theft.

Outside Area/Out of Time

Vehicle out-of-area, as well as out-of-time alarms

Maintenance Alarms

The Vehicle’s maintenance alarms are based on the travelled kilometers, preventive maintenance action control in vehicles to avoid breakdowns. Almost all changes made in vehicles are under a determined number of kilometers travelled, parameter which is easily measurable by the system. Oil changes, wheel changes, periodic revision(s), etc.

Unexpected Motion

Immediate notification if the vehicle moves unexpectedly overnight, during weekends or between the no-activity hours designated.

Arrival and Departure Notifications

It’s the notification about the vehicle’s arrival or departure, from a geographic area of interest such as headquarters, warehouse, work sites, customer sites, delivery sites, etc.

Speed Limit Exceeded

Receive alerts when the speed limit previously configured is exceeded.
